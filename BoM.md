## Bill of Materials

Make sure you have an Arduino or equivalent microprocessor, big enough sheets of 3mm to cut the design from and plenty of wires and M3 10 screws and nuts to assemble all the parts.

* As a rotor we wil use a 3D print of your own design, or this [Microfuge 8 Place Rotor](http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:97524)
